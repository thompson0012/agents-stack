# Product Blueprint

## Placement
This is a nested child under `greenfield-product`; its path is `greenfield-product/product-blueprint/`, and the router selects it before standalone use.

Use this skill to turn a raw idea into a credible product strategy, MVP boundary, and durable product intent.

## Core contract

- Stay at strategy and blueprint level.
- Do not write PRDs, requirements, or implementation design.
- If the real question is whether the business survives, route to `greenfield-product/startup-pressure-test` first.
- Produce a blueprint that can become `.agents-stack/reference/design.md`.

## Focus

Cover the minimum that makes the direction honest:

- target user and problem
- why now / why this
- MVP boundary
- key differentiators
- major risks and assumptions
- next validation steps

## Output

Return a concise strategy / blueprint artifact with clear boundaries and open questions.
