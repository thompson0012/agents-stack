# Medium & Rendering Terms

Quick reference for art styles, photography genres, rendering modes, and digital looks for text-to-image prompt engineering.

## Art Styles

- **Oil paint** — Thick impasto or blended; rich color, texture, classical to contemporary
- **Watercolor** — Transparent washes; wet-on-wet bleeding, paper texture, luminous
- **Charcoal** — Carbon stick; gritty, smudged, high contrast, expressive
- **Pencil** — Graphite; fine lines, hatching, detailed, precise to sketchy
- **Pastel** — Pigment stick; soft edges, vibrant, chalky texture, impressionistic
- **Ink** — Pen or brush; linework, wash, sumi-e, graphic, high contrast
- **Gouache** — Opaque watercolor; matte finish, flat color, illustrative
- **Acrylic** — Fast-drying polymer; bold color, flat or textured, modern
- **Fresco** — Pigment on wet plaster; muted tones, monumental, historical
- **Digital paint** — Tablet-created; unlimited, blending trad and digital effects

## Photography Genres

- **Fashion editorial** — Styled, dramatic, trend-focused; studio or location
- **Portrait** — Person-centered; environmental, studio, candid, fine-art
- **Street** — Candid urban life; decisive moment, unposed, documentary
- **Wildlife** — Animals in habitat; patience, telephoto, natural behavior
- **Macro** — Extreme detail; insects, textures, miniature worlds
- **Astro** — Night sky; star trails, Milky Way, long exposure, celestial
- **Architecture** — Built environment; structural, interior, exterior, abstract
- **Product** — Commercial object photography; clean, lit, lifestyle or studio
- **Food** — Culinary styling; overhead, 45°, texture, steam, props
- **Flat lay** — Overhead arrange-on-surface; lifestyle, product, editorial
- **Lifestyle** — Real-life context; aspirational, candid-feel, branded

## Rendering Styles

- **Photorealistic** — Indistinguishable from photo; ray-traced, PBR materials
- **Cinematic render** — Filmic color, anamorphic lens effects, narrative composition
- **CGI** — Computer-generated imagery; 3D modeled, lit, textured
- **VFX** — Visual effects compositing; integration of live-action and CG
- **Concept art** — Pre-production design; speed-paint, iterative, mood-focused
- **Matte painting** — Painted environment; film backdrop, seamless CG extension
- **Architectural viz** — Building visualizations; realistic or stylized interiors/exteriors
- **Medical viz** — Biological illustration; anatomical, cellular, procedural
- **Scientific viz** — Data and phenomena; infographics, simulation, speculative

## Digital Look

- **Lo-fi** — Low fidelity; degraded, tape, VHS aesthetic, imperfection
- **Pixel art** — Grid-based raster; retro gaming, 8-bit/16-bit, limited palette
- **Voxel** — 3D pixel blocks; Minecraft-like, volumetric, isometric
- **Vector** — Resolution-independent paths; clean edges, flat, scalable
- **Glitch** — Digital errors; corruption, data moshing, pixel sorting
- **Retro analog** — Pre-digital feel; CRT, scanlines, film grain, warmth
- **Synth-wave** — 80s retro-future; neon grids, chrome, sunset gradients
