# Color & Mood Terms

Quick reference for color theory, temperature, grading cues, emotional associations, and film stocks for text-to-image prompt engineering.

## Color Theory

- **Complementary** — Opposite on color wheel (red/green, blue/orange); maximum contrast, vibrant
- **Analogous** — Adjacent on wheel (blue/blue-green/green); harmonious, cohesive, soothing
- **Monochromatic** — Single hue varied in saturation and value; unified, elegant, subtle
- **Split-complementary** — Base plus two neighbors of its complement; contrast with less tension
- **Triadic** — Three equidistant hues (red/yellow/blue); balanced vibrancy, playful
- **Tetradic** — Two complementary pairs; rich, complex, requires careful balance

## Temperature

- **Warm** — Reds, oranges, yellows; comfort, energy, intimacy, sunset/fire
- **Cool** — Blues, greens, violets; calm, distance, melancholy, ice/water
- **Neutral** — Grays, browns, muted earth tones; grounded, timeless, understated

## Grading Cues

- **Teal and orange** — Blockbuster split-toning; shadows teal, highlights orange
- **Bleach bypass** — Skip bleach in film processing; desaturated, high contrast, gritty
- **Cross-processed** — Develop in wrong chemistry; color shifts, warped saturation, punk
- **Washed-out** — Lifted blacks, low contrast; faded, dreamy, nostalgic
- **Saturated** — Vivid, punchy colors; energetic, pop, tropical, advertising
- **Muted** — Desaturated earthy tones; understated, sophisticated, restrained
- **Pastel** — Low saturation, high value; soft, innocent, spring, candy
- **Neon** — Hyper-saturated brights; electric, cyberpunk, nightlife, signage
- **Vaporwave** — Pink/cyan/ purple palette; retro-digital, surreal, nostalgic internet

## Emotional Associations

- **Red** — Passion, danger, power, warmth, urgency
- **Orange** — Energy, autumn, enthusiasm, warmth, playfulness
- **Yellow** — Joy, caution, optimism, sickness, attention
- **Green** — Nature, growth, envy, tranquility, toxicity
- **Blue** — Trust, sadness, calm, cold, authority
- **Purple** — Royalty, mystery, spirituality, luxury, whimsy
- **Pink** — Romance, femininity, playfulness, softness, bubblegum
- **Black** — Elegance, death, sophistication, mystery, void
- **White** — Purity, minimalism, clinical, emptiness, light
- **Gray** — Neutrality, indifference, timelessness, melancholy

## Film Stocks

- **Kodak Portra** — Warm skin tones, fine grain, pastel highlights; wedding, portrait
- **Fujifilm Velvia** — Saturated, high contrast, vivid greens/reds; landscape, nature
- **Ilford HP5** — Classic B&W, visible grain, rich midtones; documentary, street
- **CineStill 800T** — Tungsten-balanced movie film; halation glow, neon, night city
